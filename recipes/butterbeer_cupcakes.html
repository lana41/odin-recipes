Butterbeer Cupcakes Recipe

Description

The butterbeer cupcake is inspired by the popular book and movie series Harry Potter. In the series, butterbeer was a popular beverage that the students would drink.

However, now it can be incoorporated in different desserts and drinks. In this case, we will be making butterbeer cupcakes!

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 1 Butter Golden Cake Mix Box
  • 2 Boxes of Butterscotch Instant Pudding
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/3 Cup of Butter(melted)
  • 1 Cup of Cream Soda
  • Cupcake Liners

Butterscotch Cream Filling and Icing

  • 2 Cups of Heavy Cream
  • 4 Tablepoons of Butterscotch Instant Pudding
  • 4 Tablespoons of Sugar

Steps

Making the Cupcakes

  1. Add all eggs to a mixing bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer. Beat the eggs for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Place the cake mix, 1 box of Instant Butterscotch Pudding, melted butter, and cream soda in the mixing bowl. Beat the mixture on low until everything is moistened, then turn the mixer to medium speed for about two minutes.
  3. Place cupcake liners in a muffin pan and preheat your oven to 325 degrees. Add approximately 3 tablespoons of batter to each cup.
  4. Bake the cupcakes for 13-18 minutes. The cupcakes are done when a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Butterscotch Cream

  1. Whip the heavy cream, Butterscotch instant pudding, and sugar until light and fluffy. You can do this with a hand mixer, stand mixer, or in a blender.
  2. If you plan on filling the cupcakes, follow these steps:
    1. Remove the centers of the cupcakes using a knife or a cupcake corer.
    2. Put the Butterscotch Cream in a piping bag or gallon ziplock bag. Cut off the end of the bag and pipe the cream into the center of each cupcake.
  3. Last, but not least, add more Butterscotch Cream to the piping bag if needed, and pipe it on top of the cupcakes in a swirling motion.
