Pasta Salad Recipe

Description

Pasta salad is a refesing salad that consists of your desired pasta noodles, favorite vegitable or meat toppings, and your desired dresing. However, for my recipe I will be using the most commonly used items with an added spin of my favorite items.

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz Box of Tri Color Rotini Pasta
  • 1 Bottle of Italian Dressing
  • 1/2 of an Onion Diced
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Bag of Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  • 1 Bag of Pepperoni Minis

Directions

Making the Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook until tender, 6 to 10 minutes (check the paboxckage for recommended cook time).
  3. Transfer pasta into a strainer and drain and rinse well under cold water.

Adding Toppings and Pasta

  1. Grab a bowl to mix the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Add the pasta to the bowl.
  3. Add to your hearts desire the diced onions, cut up cucumber, pepperoni minis, shredded cheese, and the italian dressing.
  4. Finally, mix everything together, eat, and store it in a sealable container in the fridge!
